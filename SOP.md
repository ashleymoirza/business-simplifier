## 2. Discovery Call Structure (Free 1-Hour)

### 2.1 Agenda
| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Introduction & rapport | 5 min | Build trust, set expectations |
| Business context | 10 min | Understand their business model, size, industry |
| Pain point exploration | 15 min | Identify manual processes, bottlenecks, frustrations |
| Opportunity mapping | 15 min | Match AI solutions to their specific needs |
| ROI estimation | 10 min | Quantify cost savings & efficiency gains |
| Next steps | 5 min | Present options, no-pressure close |

### 2.2 Discovery Questions to Ask
1. "What are the top 3 tasks your team spends the most time on each week?"
2. "Which of those tasks do you wish could be automated?"
3. "What's the biggest bottleneck in your current workflow?"
4. "Have you tried any AI tools before? What worked or didn't?"
5. "What would it mean for your business if you could save 20 hours/week?"
6. "Who else would be involved in a decision to implement AI?"
7. "What budget range are you considering for this?"

### 2.3 Call Outcome Options
- **Fit confirmed**: Send proposal within 24 hours
- **Needs more thought**: Send summary + offer to reconnect in 2 weeks
- **Not a fit**: Recommend resources / point them in right direction (leave a positive impression)

## 4. Project Delivery Pipeline

### 4.1 Stages

```
Inquiry → Discovery Call → Proposal Signed → Kickoff → Build → Review → Deploy → Handoff → Support
```

### 4.2 Stage Details

**Kickoff (Day 1)**
- Welcome email with project timeline
- Access to data / systems needed
- Set up communication channel (WhatsApp/Slack)
- Share project tracker

**Build (Days 2-8)**
- AI solution development
- Weekly progress updates (minimum)
- Client review at 50% and 90% complete

**Review (Day 5-9)**
- Client reviews working prototype
- Feedback collected and incorporated
- Maximum 2 revision rounds (unless unlimited in package)

**Deploy (Day 6-10)**
- Production deployment
- Documentation delivered
- Team training session (if included)

**Handoff & Support**
- 1-month email support (Growth)
- 3-month priority support (Professional)
- 6-month dedicated support (Enterprise)

### 4.3 Communication Cadence
| Package | Updates | Review Points | Support |
|---------|---------|---------------|---------|
| Growth | Weekly | 1 revision | Email, 1 month |
| Professional | Twice weekly | Unlimited revisions | Priority, 3 months |
| Enterprise | Daily | Unlimited revisions | Dedicated, 6 months |
